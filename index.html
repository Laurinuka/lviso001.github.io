The Woods


As time flies we're all falling and we need a place to hide. We took a little journey to the unknown and we came back changed, I can feel it in my bones. A place away from our worries and our shadows. There is a safe place in the woods, where we can start the fire. We will stay until we aren't afraid anymore. The cold night takes us to a place to escape the chill. A place where our dreams fill the sky. All I know is that it will be our home. The night and the chill become comfortable and easy. It's nice here, without all the people. Where we laugh and fill the air with our voices. Is there a way back? Nobody knows. No one wants to know, And we leave it all behind. We leave it all behind for a place to run and free your spirit. As all the weight lifts from your shoulders we fall asleep.
